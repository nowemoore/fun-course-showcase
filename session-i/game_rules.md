## Rules
1) Each player gets an **Identity Card**. Containing a name of the data type they represent and a set of abilities (things this data type can and cannot do). **Do not disclose your identity to anyone.**
2) All players mingle around for 10 minutes and ask other players **YES/NO questions** about their abilities. 
3) The goal is the to ask the right questions to be able to **deduce the data types of as many players as possible**.
4) When asked a question, **each player must answer the questions truthfully** based on the abilities stated on their Identity Card.
